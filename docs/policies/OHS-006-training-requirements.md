# OHS-006: Training Requirements

**Document ID:** OHS-006  
**Version:** 1.0  
**ISO Standard:** ISO 45001:2018 (Clause 7.2)

## Purpose
Ensure all personnel have necessary safety competence.

## Mandatory Training - All Guides

### 1. Initial Safety Induction (Before first trip)
- Company safety policy
- Emergency procedures
- Incident reporting
- Equipment use
- Communication protocols
- **Duration**: 4 hours
- **Assessment**: Pass/Fail exam (80% required)

### 2. First Aid & CPR
- Basic first aid
- CPR techniques
- AED use
- Marine injuries
- **Duration**: 16 hours
- **Certification**: Valid 2 years
- **Renewal**: Refresher course

### 3. SIM Kapal (Boat License)
- Navigation skills
- Maritime rules
- Weather interpretation
- Emergency handling
- **Certification**: Per Indonesian maritime regulation
- **Renewal**: Per regulatory requirement

### 4. ALIN Certification
- Marine environment
- Coral reef protection
- Responsible tourism
- **Duration**: 8 hours
- **Certification**: Valid per ALIN standards

### 5. Risk Assessment Training
- Hazard identification
- Risk scoring
- Using Guide App assessment tool
- Decision making
- **Duration**: 3 hours
- **Frequency**: Annual

### 6. Emergency Response Procedures
- SOS system use
- Man overboard procedures
- Medical emergency handling
- Evacuation procedures
- **Duration**: 2 hours
- **Frequency**: Semi-annual

### 7. Customer Service & Safety Briefing
- Passenger safety briefing
- Managing difficult situations
- Crisis communication
- **Duration**: 2 hours
- **Frequency**: Annual

## Training Matrix

| Role | Training | Frequency | Mandatory |
|------|----------|-----------|-----------|
| Tour Guide | All above | Per schedule | Yes |
| Operations Staff | 1, 5, 6 | Per schedule | Yes |
| Admin Staff | 1 only | At hire | Yes |
| Partners | 1, 7 | At onboarding | Yes |

## Competence Assessment

Methods:
- Written exams
- Practical demonstrations
- Observed trips (shadowing)
- Performance reviews
- Incident analysis

## Training Records

Maintained in system:
- Employee ID
- Training completed
- Date completed
- Certificate number
- Expiry date (if applicable)
- Trainer name
- Assessment result

## Refresher Training

Triggered by:
- Certification expiry approaching
- Incident involvement
- Extended absence (> 6 months)
- Procedure changes
- Poor performance

## Training Effectiveness

Evaluated through:
- Post-training assessments
- Supervisor observations
- Incident rates
- Customer feedback
- Audit findings

## Training Calendar

Automatic reminders:
- 60 days before expiry: First reminder

- 15 days before expiry: Final warning
- At expiry: Trip assignment blocked until renewed
