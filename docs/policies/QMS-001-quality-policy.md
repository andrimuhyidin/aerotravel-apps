## 1. Policy Statement

MyAeroTravel ID is committed to providing high-quality travel management services that meet or exceed customer expectations and comply with applicable regulatory requirements including ISO 9001:2015, ISO 31030:2021, ISO 27001:2021, ISO 14001:2015, and ISO 45001:2018.

Our quality policy is founded on:
- **Customer Focus**: Understanding and meeting customer needs and requirements
- **Leadership**: Establishing direction and creating conditions for employee engagement
- **Process Approach**: Managing activities and resources as processes for efficiency
- **Continuous Improvement**: Ongoing enhancement of quality management system
- **Evidence-Based Decision Making**: Using data and analysis for decisions
- **Relationship Management**: Managing relationships with interested parties

---

## 2. Quality Objectives

1. **Customer Satisfaction**: Achieve minimum 4.5/5.0 customer satisfaction rating
2. **Service Reliability**: Maintain 98% trip completion rate without incidents
3. **Response Time**: Respond to customer inquiries within 2 hours during business hours
4. **Safety Performance**: Zero critical safety incidents per quarter
5. **Environmental Performance**: Reduce carbon emissions by 5% year-over-year
6. **Compliance**: Maintain 100% compliance with regulatory requirements

---

## 3. Scope and Applicability

This policy applies to:
- All employees, contractors, and partners of MyAeroTravel ID
- All business processes including:
  - Booking and reservation management
  - Trip planning and execution
  - Guide and crew operations
  - Customer service
  - Safety and risk management
  - Environmental management
  - Information security

---

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities

### 4.1 Executive Management
- Establish and communicate quality policy
- Provide resources for QMS implementation
- Review QMS performance quarterly
- Approve major quality initiatives

### 4.2 Quality Manager
- Maintain and update QMS documentation
- Conduct internal audits
- Monitor quality metrics and KPIs
- Facilitate management reviews
- Coordinate corrective actions

### 4.3 Department Heads
- Implement quality procedures in their departments
- Monitor departmental quality metrics
- Report quality issues promptly
- Participate in quality improvement initiatives

### 4.4 All Employees
- Understand and follow quality procedures
- Report quality issues and non-conformances
- Participate in training programs
- Contribute to continuous improvement

## 6. Performance Measurement

### 6.1 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI | Target | Measurement Frequency |
|-----|--------|----------------------|
| Customer Satisfaction Score | ≥ 4.5/5.0 | Monthly |
| Trip Completion Rate | ≥ 98% | Weekly |
| Safety Incident Rate | 0 critical incidents | Monthly |
| Customer Complaint Response Time | ≤ 2 hours | Daily |
| Non-Conformance Closure Rate | ≥ 95% within 30 days | Monthly |
