# QMS-005: Internal Audit

**Document ID:** QMS-005  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** January 4, 2026  
**Owner:** Quality Manager  
**ISO Standard:** ISO 9001:2015 (Clause 9.2)

---

## 1. Purpose

Establish systematic internal audit program to verify QMS conforms to requirements and is effectively implemented and maintained.

---

## 2. Audit Program

### 2.1 Audit Schedule

| Process/Department | Frequency | ISO Clause |
|-------------------|-----------|------------|
| Quality Management System | Annual | All |
| Operations & Trip Management | Semi-annual | 8.1-8.7 |
| Guide Safety & Compliance | Quarterly | 8.1, 9.1 |
| Customer Service | Semi-annual | 8.2, 9.1 |
| Partner Management | Annual | 8.4 |
| Information Security | Semi-annual | ISO 27001 |
| Environmental Management | Semi-annual | ISO 14001 |
| Health & Safety | Quarterly | ISO 45001 |

### 2.2 Audit Types

**Planned Audits**:
- Scheduled in annual audit program
- All areas audited within audit cycle

**Special Audits**:
- Triggered by: Major non-conformances, customer complaints, regulatory changes, management request

---

## 3. Audit Team

### 3.1 Auditor Qualifications

- Internal Auditor Training (ISO 19011)
- Knowledge of audit standards
- Independence from audited area
- Communication skills

### 3.2 Lead Auditor Responsibilities

- Plan audit
- Conduct opening/closing meetings
- Lead audit team
- Report findings
- Follow up on corrective actions

---

## 4. Audit Process

### Phase 1: Planning (2 weeks before)
- Define audit scope and criteria
- Review previous audit reports
- Prepare audit checklist
- Notify auditee

### Phase 2: Execution
- Opening meeting
- Document review
- Process observation
- Interviews
- Evidence collection
- Daily debrief

### Phase 3: Reporting (Within 5 days)
- Closing meeting
- Draft report
- Management review
- Final report issuance

### Phase 4: Follow-up (30-60 days)
- Corrective action plans submitted
- Implementation verification
- Effectiveness check
- Close findings

---

## 5. Audit Findings Classification

### 5.1 Major Non-Conformance
- Requirements not met
- Systemic failure
- Risk to quality/safety
- Action: Immediate correction + root cause analysis

### 5.2 Minor Non-Conformance
- Isolated issue
- Low risk
- Action: Correction within 30 days

### 5.3 Observation/Opportunity for Improvement
- Not a non-conformance
- Potential for enhancement
- Action: Consider for improvement

---

## 6. Corrective Actions

Required for all non-conformances:
1. Immediate correction
2. Root cause analysis
3. Corrective action plan
4. Implementation
5. Verification
6. Effectiveness review
