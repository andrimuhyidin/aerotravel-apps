# QMS-004: Training and Competence

**Document ID:** QMS-004  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** January 4, 2026  
**Owner:** HR Manager / Quality Manager  
**ISO Standard:** ISO 9001:2015 (Clause 7.2)

---

## 1. Purpose

Ensure all personnel are competent based on appropriate education, training, skills, and experience to perform work affecting quality.

---

## 2. Training Requirements

### 2.1 Mandatory Training (All Employees)

| Training | Frequency | Duration |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| Quality System Orientation | At hire | 2 hours |
| Information Security Awareness | Annual | 1 hour |
| Data Protection (GDPR/PDP) | Annual | 1 hour |
| Safety Procedures | Annual | 2 hours |

### 2.2 Role-Specific Training

**Tour Guides**:
- First Aid & CPR (refresher every 2 years)
- SIM Kapal (renewal per regulation)
- ALIN Certification (as required)
- Customer Service Excellence
- Risk Assessment Procedures
- Emergency Response Protocols

**Operations Staff**:
- Trip Planning & Logistics
- Booking System Usage
- Customer Complaint Handling
- Safety Management System

**Partners (Mitra)**:
- Partner Portal Training
- Booking Management
- Compliance Requirements
- Whitelabel Configuration

---

## 3. Competence Assessment

### 3.1 Methods
- Knowledge tests (minimum 70% pass)
- Practical demonstrations
- On-the-job observations
- Performance reviews
- Customer feedback

### 3.2 Frequency
- Initial: Before independent work
- Periodic: Annual review
- After major changes: As needed

---

## 4. Training Records

All training shall be documented with:
- Employee name and ID
- Training title and code
- Date completed
- Training duration
- Trainer name
- Assessment result
- Certificate (if applicable)

**Retention**: Duration of employment + 5 years

---

## 5. Training Effectiveness Evaluation

Within 90 days of training:
- Performance monitoring
- Supervisor assessment
- Error rate analysis
- Customer feedback review

---

## 6. Continuous Professional Development

- Minimum 16 hours per year
- Industry conferences/workshops
- Online courses
- Certifications
